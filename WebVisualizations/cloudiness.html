Latitude vs. Cloudiness

Summary

When measured against a cities latitude, cloudiness does not appear to show any linear trend in either direction away from the equator. Most of the data points are clumped around multiples of 10%, with the majority of these close to 0 or 100%.

This is probably because cloudiness is more of an estimated metric. It is likely attributed to things such as time of year or yearly rainfall in a given city.
