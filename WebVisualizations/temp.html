Latitude vs. Temperature

Summary

This plot shows latitude of a city versus max temperature on a given date. The left side of the graph with negative latitude represents cities in the southern hemisphere and positive latitude represents cities in the northern hemisphere.

The max temperature clearly becomes higher the closer you get to the equator. The northern hemisphere tends to have lower overall temperatures because which could be due to the greater amount of cities at extreme latitudes.
